Scheme: PM Matsya Sampada Yojana
Eligibility: All marine and inland fishermen, fish farmers, fishing cooperatives
Benefit: Subsidy up to 60% for SC/ST/women, 40% for others on boats, nets, ice boxes, cold storage
Financial limit: Up to 3 lakh rupees for motorized boats
How to apply: Through state fisheries department portal or nearest fisheries office
Contact: pmmsy.dof.gov.in
